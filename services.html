What We Do

More Than Just a Taproom

Explore our curated experiences crafted for beer enthusiasts, party hosts, and casual weekend visitors alike.

Guided Brewery Tours

Go behind the scenes with our head brewmaster. Learn about our grain-to-glass process, see our fermentation tanks up close, and enjoy an exclusive tasting session right off the brite tanks.

View Details

Private Event Hosting

Looking for a unique venue? Rent out our barrel room or outdoor patio for your next corporate gathering, birthday, or private party. We offer flexible floor plans and custom open-bar packages.

View Details

Commercial Keg Sales

Serve GrowwBrew at your own establishment. We offer 1/2 BBL and 1/6 BBL kegs for local bars and restaurants, complete with branded tap handles, swift delivery, and line cleaning support.

Mug Club Membership

Join our inner circle. Members receive a custom numbered mug kept on-site, larger pours for the standard price, early access to limited can releases, and exclusive invites to VIP brewery events.

Curated Tasting Flights

Can't decide on just one? Build your own flight board. Choose any four 5oz pours from our current tap list, perfectly arranged and explained from light to dark by our knowledgeable staff.
