More Than Just Beer

The Taproom Experience

We believe that great beer is best enjoyed in a great environment. Our taproom was built to be a community hub—a place to gather, celebrate, and unwind. From our scratch kitchen to behind-the-scenes tours, there’s always something brewing.

Artisan Kitchen

Our menu is designed specifically to pair with our beers. Wood-fired pizzas, gourmet pretzels with beer cheese, and seasonal shared plates made from locally sourced ingredients.

Brewery Tours

Get a behind-the-scenes look at our brewhouse. Learn about the brewing process from grain to glass, meet our head brewer, and enjoy an exclusive tasting straight from the bright tanks.

Dog-Friendly Patio

Bring your furry friends! Our expansive outdoor beer garden features fire pits, lawn games, and plenty of water bowls. The perfect spot to soak up the sun with a cold pint.
