The Process

How The Membership Works

We've made joining the GrowwBrew family as seamless as possible. No complicated contracts, no hidden fees. Just great beer and a great community.

1

Sign Up Online

Choose your tier above and complete your registration via our secure online portal. Your digital membership card is generated instantly.

2

Claim Your Mug

Visit the taproom on your next free day. Show your digital card to the bartender to be assigned your custom numbered ceramic mug.

3

Enjoy The Perks

Start receiving larger pours, taproom discounts, and email alerts for exclusive bottle releases. Your mug will always be clean and waiting for you.

4

Renew or Cancel

Memberships are valid for one full calendar year. We will send you a reminder 30 days before renewal. Cancel anytime with zero hassle.

Got Questions?

Membership FAQs

Find answers to the most common questions regarding our taproom memberships.

Mug Club memberships and their associated perks (like 20oz pours and merchandise discounts) are strictly non-transferable and apply only to the registered member. However, you are always welcome to buy your friend a standard pint!
Accidents happen! If our staff accidentally breaks your mug, we will replace it immediately at no cost to you. If a member drops and breaks their own mug, a $20 replacement fee applies to fire a new custom numbered mug.
The 4 exclusive barrel-aged bottles are released quarterly (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter). Barrel Master members will receive an email two weeks prior to the release date with tasting notes and pickup window instructions.
Yes! If you start as a Casual Sipper and realize you want the Mug Club Pro benefits, you can speak to a manager in the taproom or email us to prorate your upgrade for the remainder of your annual billing cycle.
Your free birthday pint can be claimed any day during your birthday week (3 days before or 3 days after your actual birthday). Just show your ID to the bartender to verify the date, and your first pour is on the house!
