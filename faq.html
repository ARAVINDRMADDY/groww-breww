The Basics

Visiting the Taproom

Everything you need to know before you grab a pint at our facility.

While we do not have an in-house kitchen, we partner with a rotating schedule of local food trucks that park right outside our patio every Thursday through Sunday. You are also more than welcome to bring your own food, order delivery directly to the taproom, or bring snacks from home!
Yes! We pride ourselves on being a family-friendly establishment. Children are welcome in the taproom as long as they are accompanied by a parent or guardian. Well-behaved, leashed dogs are strictly permitted on our outdoor patio area, but cannot come inside the main taproom due to health department regulations.
For standard taproom visits, seating is purely first-come, first-served. However, if you have a group of 15 or more, please contact our event coordinator at least 48 hours in advance, and we will do our best to block off a section of tables for your party.
Absolutely. While all of our house-brewed beers contain gluten, we maintain a dedicated tap line for a locally sourced hard cider, and we stock a variety of gluten-free canned wines. For non-alcoholic options, we offer house-made craft sodas, sparkling water, and cold-brew coffee on nitro.
We have a dedicated 30-spot parking lot directly behind the brewery building. If our lot is full, there is ample free street parking along Brewer's Lane and the adjacent cross streets. We strongly encourage ridesharing if you plan on sampling heavily!
Beyond the Pint

Memberships, Tours & Kegs

Details regarding our premium services and bulk sales.

Mug Club sign-ups open twice a year (January and July) and spots are highly limited due to physical shelf space for the ceramic mugs. You can secure your spot by navigating to our Pricing page during an open enrollment period, or by speaking to a bartender on-site.
Yes! We offer Private Group Buyouts for corporate team-building events, bachelor/bachelorette parties, and large family gatherings. A private excursion allows you to secure the entire tour block and comes with customizable tasting menus. Please reach out via our Contact page for availability.
We certainly do. Kegs are available for public purchase for weddings, backyard BBQs, and personal kegerators. We require a 72-hour notice for all keg orders to ensure the specific beer is in stock and ready. A fully refundable $50 deposit is required for the stainless steel keg shell.
All of our kegs use standard American "D-System" (Sankey) couplers. If you do not have your own kegerator or jockey box, you can rent a standard party pump (hand pump) and a plastic ice tub from us for an additional $15 fee.

The vast majority of our facility, including the taproom, patio, and main brewhouse floor, is fully ADA compliant and wheelchair accessible. However, the upper mezzanine where the milling room is located requires climbing a set of stairs. If a guest cannot access this area, the guide will bring the grain samples down to the main floor.
